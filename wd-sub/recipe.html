Brownies Recipe

  1. Prep Work

    • Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
    • Rest the butter to room temperature
    • Wash your hands

  2. Mix dry ingredients

    1. In a large mixing bowl, add 2 1/2 cups of all-purpose flower, 1 cup of brown sugar, and 1 cup of cocoa powder. Stir ingredients thoroughly.
    2. Add in 1 tablespoon of baking powder. Also 1 teaspoon of baking soda.
    3. Set aside.

  3. Mix wet ingredients

    1. In a medium mixing bowl, break 2 large eggs. Whisk until consistent.
    2. Add one tablespoon of vanilla extract and a half teaspoon of salt to the egg mixture.
    3. Pour a half cup of milk into the wet mix.

  4. Combine mixtures

    1. Place the large mixing bowl with the dry ingredients in a stand mixer on low setting.
    2. Slowly pour wet mixture in until incorporated.
    3. Drop 8 tbsp of room temperature butter into the mixer. Consistently should be fluid but slow.

  5. Pour and bake

    1. Grease pan
    2. Pour mixture into pan, and smooth it out with spatula.
    3. Place in oven and bake for 20 minutes or until toothpick is clean.
